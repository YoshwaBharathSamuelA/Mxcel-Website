ABOUT MXCEL

MXCEL is a national-level technical symposium conducted annually by the Mechanical Engineering Association (MEA). Our flagship event continues to inspire countless ideas, expedite innovative startups, and host a jam-packed cluster of events designed for everyone. MXCEL 2024, scheduled for October 9th, aims to propel these ideas to new heights, because we know that great ideas are hard to come by!

ABOUT KEC

Kongu Engineering College, affiliated with Anna University, is situated in Perundurai, Erode. It holds an A++ grade accreditation from NAAC. Since its establishment in 1984, the institution has grown into a center of excellence with robust infrastructure, outstanding academic performance, and a serene learning environment that shapes future leaders.
